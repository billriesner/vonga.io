MLS Is Growing — But Most Clubs Don't Know Their Fans

Attendance is up. Expansion is accelerating. Fan data infrastructure is lagging.

The Gap

MLS supporter culture is among the most passionate in American sports. Tifo, supporter groups, scarf culture, match day rituals — the energy is real.

But most of that passion is anonymous to the front office. You see them in the stands. You don't know who they are.

The Opportunity

MLS fans are younger, more diverse, and more mobile-first than fans of other major leagues. They're open to new experiences.

That demographic profile is perfect for NFC engagement — frictionless, phone-native, and instant.
