The College Fan Engagement Problem

Your student section is packed. Your student database is empty.

The Challenge

  • Students are your most passionate fans — and most anonymous
  • 4-year engagement window before they graduate and disappear
  • Students won't download another app
  • Email open rates are under 10%

The Stakes

The donor pipeline starts with fan identity. Students who feel connected to athletics during school become donors after.

But if you can't identify them as students, you can't engage them as alumni. Most programs have zero fan data for the students filling their stadiums.

From Game Day Fan to Lifetime Donor

The engagement pipeline that turns students into giving alumni.

During Enrollment (4 Years)

  1. Year 1: Capture identity at freshman orientation or first game
  2. Year 2-3: Track engagement across sports, events, and seasons
  3. Year 4: Rich fan profile built from 4 years of behavioral data

After Graduation

  1. Handoff: Rich fan profiles sent to alumni/development office
  2. Targeted asks: Engagement history informs giving solicitation
  3. Better conversion: 10x more effective than "update your info" email blasts
