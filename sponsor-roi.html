The Problem

Stadium sponsorships are expensive. Proving they worked has always been nearly impossible.

You're flying blind on activation ROI

You spend six or seven figures on stadium activations. At season's end, your agency sends a PowerPoint with estimated impressions based on attendance headcounts. That's not attribution — that's a guess multiplied by another guess.

Estimated impressions

Based on total attendance — not actual fan interaction with your brand

No fan demographics

You don't know if you reached your target audience or just filled a stadium

End-of-season lag

By the time you get data, renewal decisions have already been made on gut feel

The Vonga Solution

NFC-enabled merchandise creates a direct, verified data channel between your activation and the fan.

Every tap is a measurable engagement event

When a fan taps their NFC-enabled jersey, hat, or other branded merch item, Vonga logs a verified engagement event — complete with timestamp, location, and device type. Not an estimate. A real person, in a real place, engaging with your brand at a specific moment.

That data flows into a live sponsor portal you can access anytime, share with your team, and hand directly to your CMO to justify next year's budget.

How it creates the data channel

1

NFC chip embeds in merch at production

Your branded items — jerseys, caps, scarves — carry an NFC tag during production. No change to look or feel.

2

Fan taps — engagement logged instantly

No app needed. No friction. Phone to tag, browser opens, engagement recorded.

3

Live portal updates in real time

Your sponsor portal updates as fans engage — during the game, during the season, across every activation.
