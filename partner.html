Integration Options

We meet you where you are. From simple chip embeds to full API connectivity — pick the model that fits your operation.

Most Common

Embed

Order pre-encoded Vonga NFC chips. Your production team adds them during the normal manufacturing run. No software integration needed. Ship smarter merchandise immediately.

Best for: Apparel manufacturers, decorators, screen printers, embroidery shops

Technical Partners

API

Connect your order management or fulfillment platform to the Vonga API. Push product metadata, automate reporting, and give your clients a seamless data-driven experience.

Best for: Distributors with existing platforms, large-scale fulfillment operations, tech-forward suppliers

Strategic

Co-Branded

Joint go-to-market. Your brand + Vonga on the product label and in team pitches. Shared marketing, co-developed sales materials, and co-sell opportunities on target accounts in your territory.

Best for: National distributors, exclusive suppliers for specific leagues or conferences
