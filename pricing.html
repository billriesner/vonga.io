Common Questions

What does the platform cost?

Starter: $995/month ($10,000/year). Professional: $2,495/month ($25,000/year). Enterprise: custom starting at $5,000/month. All include hosting, analytics, support, and ongoing updates.

Can we start small and scale up?

Yes. Start with Starter for a single program. Move to Professional when you expand across sports. Upgrade anytime with no penalties or setup fees.

How does apparel work?

We supply NFC-enabled premium apparel separately. You set your pricing and control margins. Most programs add $10-25 premium per garment and maintain 60%+ margins.

Is there a long-term contract?

Annual plans offer best value (2 months free). Monthly options available at slightly higher rates. Cancel anytime with 30-day notice. No auto-renewal surprises.

What if we need custom features?

Professional includes quarterly strategy sessions. Enterprise includes custom development and integrations. Additional features available across all tiers.

How do we measure success?

All plans include real-time analytics: tap rates, content performance, geographic distribution, and audience insights. We help you set KPIs and track ROI together.
