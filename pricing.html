Common Questions

Do we have to change our merch supplier?

No. We supply pre-encoded NFC chips designed to integrate into standard merch production: sew-in labels, hang tags, or heat-seal patches depending on the product. Your supplier's process stays the same. We handle the technology.

Is there a long-term contract?

The pilot is a 3-month commitment, enough time to prove ROI. After that, annual plans offer the best value (~15% discount). Monthly billing is available at the standard rates shown. Annual contracts renew with 30-day notice.

What does the fan see when they tap?

Content you configure through the dashboard: a video from your head coach, an exclusive offer, a behind-the-scenes clip. You control the experience. The fan sees your brand, not ours.

How does the sponsor portal work?

Each sponsor gets a shareable link to their portal: live engagement data from their specific activation. Who interacted, how many times, what they did next. No more manually building reports.

Can we upgrade tiers later?

Yes. Upgrade anytime. Your fan profiles and historical data move with you. There are no migration fees or setup costs for tier changes.

What about CRM or ticketing integration?

API access and webhooks are available on Playbook and Enterprise tiers. Growth includes data export in CSV/JSON. We'll work with you to connect Vonga data into your existing systems.
