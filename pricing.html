Common Questions

Do we have to change our merch supplier?

No. You keep your existing supplier — Fanatics, Nike, New Era, a local shop, whoever. We supply the NFC chips that get embedded during their production process. Their job doesn't change.

What's included in the chip allocation?

Each plan includes a yearly allocation of garment-grade NFC chips (NTAG 424 DNA), pre-encoded and production-ready. Additional chips beyond your allocation are $3–5 per chip depending on volume.

Is there a long-term contract?

Annual plans offer the best value (roughly 7% discount). Monthly billing is available at the standard rates shown. Annual contracts renew with 30-day notice before the renewal date.

What does the fan see when they tap?

Whatever you build in the campaign builder. A video from your head coach, an exclusive offer, a poll, a behind-the-scenes clip. You control the experience. The fan sees content — not an ad for Vonga.

How does the sponsor portal work?

Each sponsor gets a shareable link to their portal — live engagement data from their specific activation. Who interacted, how many times, what they did next. They can log in themselves. No more manually building reports.

Can we upgrade tiers later?

Yes. Upgrade anytime — your fan profiles and historical data move with you. There are no migration fees or setup costs for tier changes.

What if we want to integrate with our ticketing system?

API access and custom integrations (Archtics, Paciolan, SeatGeek, Salesforce) are available on Arena and Stadium. Playbook includes data export in CSV/JSON for manual integration workflows.

How are the chips installed in our merch?

We ship pre-encoded chips in a format that's ready for your supplier's production line — sew-in labels, hang tags, or heat-sealable patches depending on the product type. We walk your supplier through it. Usually takes one production run to establish the workflow.
