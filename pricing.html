Common Questions

How much does it cost?

Pricing is scoped to your specific activation — the item type, volume, and season length. Talk to us and we'll give you a number specific to what you're planning. Most teams are surprised how accessible the entry point is.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Single activations are one invoice, one engagement. Season packages run for your season. Annual programs renew with 30-day notice. You're never locked in before the value is proven.

How does pricing work when a sponsor is funding the activation?

When a sponsor co-funds an activation, pricing is structured around their program budget rather than the team's budget. We work with both parties. Talk to us about co-funded activation structures.

Can we start with just one activation?

That's how most teams start. One item. One campaign. Real data. If it changes your next renewal conversation — and it usually does — we talk about what's next.

Do we have to change our merch supplier?

No. Vonga chips are embedded during normal production by your existing supplier. Nothing in their process changes.
