The Fan Intelligence Gap

Your fan database knows what fans do online. It knows almost nothing about what they do in the physical world.

What You Know Today

  • Which fans bought tickets (ticket scan data)
  • Which fans open your emails (email platform data)
  • Which fans follow you on social (social analytics)
  • Which fans use your app (app analytics — if anyone downloads it)

This is a digital-only picture. It misses everyone who engages with your brand through physical touchpoints.

What You're Missing

  • Which fans wear your gear — and where
  • Which fans engage with your merch after game day
  • Which fans are most loyal based on physical behavior
  • Which fans your sponsors are actually reaching

Physical fan touchpoints are your largest channel. They generate the most real-world engagement. And they contribute zero data to your fan database today.

Behavioral Fan Segmentation

Slice your fan database by engagement score, tap frequency, geographic location, content preference, and item type. Build campaigns that reach the right fans with the right message at the right moment.
