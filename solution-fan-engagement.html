The Engagement Crisis

Traditional methods aren't working. Digital-native fans expect more.

Social Media Is Failing

2 to 3% organic reach on major platforms. Your content gets buried in algorithms. Fans scroll past without engaging.

  • Posts reach a fraction of followers
  • Engagement rates declining year over year
  • Paid promotion required for visibility
  • No way to break through the noise

Fans Are Passive

One-way communication doesn't build loyalty. Fans consume content but don't participate. No emotional investment.

  • A like does not equal engagement
  • No memorable moments created
  • Weak emotional connection
  • Easy to forget, easier to ignore

Gen Z Expects Different

Digital-native fans want interactive, personalized experiences. Traditional marketing feels impersonal and corporate.

  • Expect technology integration
  • Want shareable moments
  • Demand authenticity
  • Skip generic content

No Clear Path Forward

Trying everything, mastering nothing. Social, email, apps. None are moving the needle. Budgets wasted on experiments.

  • App downloads are abysmal
  • Email open rates declining

  • Guessing what fans want
