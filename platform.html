Four Systems. One Platform.

Each one solves a problem that teams currently handle with spreadsheets, estimates, or nothing at all.

Fan Profiles

Every tap creates or enriches a fan profile automatically. No form fills, no app downloads, no opt-in walls. Profiles build over time, across items, events, and seasons.

Replaces: nothing. Teams don't have this data today.

Sponsor ROI Portal

Each sponsor gets a shareable link to their own live dashboard. Verified engagement counts, fan demographics, content performance, updated in real time, not at end-of-season.

Replaces: PowerPoint decks with estimated impressions.

Campaign Builder

Design what fans see when they tap: video, offers, polls, exclusive content. Swap experiences by event, by item, or by time of day. No code, no developer tickets.

Replaces: static QR codes that link to one thing forever.

AI Insights

The platform watches your data and surfaces what matters: which items drive the most engagement, which campaigns underperform, which fan segments to target next. Recommendations, not just reports.

Replaces: manual analysis across disconnected systems.

Who Uses This and Why

Game Day Giveaways

Distribute NFC-enabled items on opening night. Fans tap during the game, after the game, and in the weeks that follow. Each tap builds a profile. The giveaway keeps generating data long after the event.

Sponsor Renewals

Walk into the meeting with a live portal instead of a slide deck. Sponsors see their own verified engagement data: taps, demographics, content performance, and close on what they see, not what you estimate.

University Athletics

Embed chips in student section giveaways. Build fan profiles that persist after graduation. Your alumni office gets engagement data they've never had, from physical merch, not a form fill.

Retail Jerseys

Embed chips in jerseys sold at retail. Every buyer who taps enters the fan database, giving you first-party data from a channel that currently returns zero. The jersey keeps generating insights for years.
