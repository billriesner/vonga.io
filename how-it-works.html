Technical Details

Vonga NFC chips use NTAG 424 DNA technology with AES-128 encryption. No battery required — powered by the phone's NFC field. Chip form factor (sew-in, hang tag, heat-seal patch, or sticker) is matched to the product type. Compatible with iPhone 7+ and Android 5.0+ (94% of smartphones). Content loads in seconds via native browser — no app download required.

Fan-facing content is cloud-based and can be updated anytime without touching the physical product. Supports video, offers, and sponsor activations. Marketing teams manage content through the dashboard.

Each tap captures location, time, device, and engagement behavior. The fan database includes segmentation by behavior and location, real-time geographic heatmaps, and live tap activity feeds during events.

Sponsor portals are auto-generated with verified engagement counts, fan demographics, engagement timelines, geographic reach, content click-through data, and one-click export for sponsor reporting.

Common Questions

Does it require an app?

No. Uses the phone's built-in NFC — same as Apple Pay. Tap and a page opens in the browser. Seconds.

How durable is the chip?

NTAG 424 DNA — waterproof, no battery, AES-128 encrypted. Same chip standard used in luxury brand authentication. Form factor matched to your product.

Do fans need an account?

No. Data is collected through behavior — what they tap, when, where, how often. No forms, no signup, no friction.

Can we change content after distribution?

Yes. Content is cloud-based. A jersey from October delivers new content in January. Update instantly — the physical product never changes.

How fast is onboarding?

Designed for fast onboarding. Dashboard access is immediate. Full program launch — including chip provisioning and campaign setup — typically takes weeks, not months.

What phones work with it?

iPhone 7+ and Android 5.0+ with NFC. Covers roughly 94% of smartphones in active use. No setup required on the fan's end.
