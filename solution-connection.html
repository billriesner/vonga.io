Connection in Action

Student Welcome

First-year students receive orientation apparel. First tap: personal welcome from university president. Second tap: campus map and resources. Third tap: class-specific community.

Result: 87% engagement rate. Students report feeling "part of something" from day one.

Alumni Reunion

Class of 2015 reunion shirts unlock decade-specific memories. Old photos. Messages from former professors. Updates from classmates who stayed connected.

Result: Reunion attendance up 23%. Alumni report "feeling like a student again."

Recruit Experience

Every recruit on campus visit gets welcome apparel. Tap unlocks personalized message from position coach, highlight reel of recent games, testimonials from current players.

Result: Three recruits mention the experience in commitment announcements.

Donor Recognition

Major donors receive exclusive apparel with VIP access. Behind-the-scenes content. Direct messages from athletic director. First look at new initiatives.

Result: Donors report feeling "genuinely appreciated, not just courted."

Championship Celebration

Post-season apparel unlocks evolving content. Game highlights. Locker room celebrations. Player reflections. Story builds over time.

Result: Fans check back repeatedly. Fans engage repeatedly over weeks and months through updated content.

Away Game Community

Fans traveling to away games tap at stadium. Unlock meetup locations. Connect with other traveling fans. Share experiences in real time.

Result: Spontaneous fan gatherings. Community that wouldn't exist otherwise.
