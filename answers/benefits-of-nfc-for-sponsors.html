What Are the Benefits of NFC for Sports Sponsors?

The primary benefit of NFC for sports sponsors is the ability to prove Return on Investment (ROI) with verifiable data. Instead of estimating impressions, sponsors can track every single fan tap on their branded merchandise or in-venue signage.

Other key benefits include:

  • Direct Fan Engagement: NFC opens a direct channel to fans, allowing sponsors to deliver targeted content, offers, and surveys.
  • First-Party Data: Sponsors can build their own database of engaged fans, which is critical as third-party cookies are phased out.
  • Persistent Activation: A sponsored giveaway item with an NFC chip continues to generate engagement and data long after the game ends.
