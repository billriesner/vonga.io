How Does NFC Fan Engagement Work?

NFC fan engagement works in three simple steps: An NFC chip is embedded into an item (like a jersey or giveaway). A fan taps their smartphone near the item. The chip directs the phone's browser to a specific digital experience (like exclusive content or an offer), and the interaction is captured as a data point.

The process is seamless because it uses the phone's built-in NFC reader, eliminating the need for app downloads or camera scanning. This low-friction experience leads to higher engagement rates compared to traditional methods like QR codes. For the team or sponsor, each tap provides valuable, real-time data on who is engaging, where, and when.
