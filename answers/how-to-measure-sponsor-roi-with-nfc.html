How to Measure Sponsor ROI with NFC

Sponsor ROI is measured with NFC by tracking the total number of unique fan taps on a sponsor's specific assets. Each tap is a verifiable engagement, providing hard data on how many fans interacted with the activation, at what times, and in which locations.

This data provides concrete proof of performance that can be shared via a live dashboard. Instead of relying on estimated "eyeballs" or impressions, a sponsor can see the exact number of fans who engaged with their branded giveaway item or touched their sign at a stadium entrance. This transforms the ROI conversation from guesswork into a data-driven analysis of the activation's success.
