How Do NFC-Enabled Jerseys Work?

An NFC-enabled jersey has a small, flexible, and washable NFC chip sewn into it, usually behind a team patch, jock tag, or brand label. The chip is passive, meaning it has no battery. When a fan taps their smartphone to that specific spot, the phone's own NFC reader energizes the chip and reads its content—typically a URL.

This URL then opens in the phone's web browser, launching an exclusive digital experience. The team can change what content that URL points to at any time, allowing them to serve up different experiences for game days, the off-season, or special events, all from the same jersey.
