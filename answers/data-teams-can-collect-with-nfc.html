What Data Can Teams Collect with NFC?

Sports teams using NFC can collect valuable first-party data with each fan tap. This data primarily falls into three categories: engagement data (which product was tapped, when, and how often), location data (the geographic location of the tap), and device data (such as the type of smartphone used).

When a fan voluntarily provides information to access more exclusive content or offers, the team can also collect zero-party data like a name, email address, or favorite player. Over time, these individual data points combine to build a comprehensive and actionable profile for each fan, all owned directly by the team.
