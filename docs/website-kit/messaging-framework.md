# Messaging Framework — Sports Team Focus

## ⚠️ CRITICAL RULE: NO EM DASHES

**NEVER use em dashes (—) in any copy.**

Em dashes clutter copy and reduce readability. Always use:
- **Periods** for complete thoughts
- **Commas** for connecting clauses
- **Parentheses** for asides

❌ **Wrong:** "No apps needed — just tap"  
✅ **Right:** "No apps needed. Just tap."

❌ **Wrong:** "Turn apparel into connection — everywhere fans go"  
✅ **Right:** "Turn apparel into connection, everywhere fans go."

This rule applies to ALL copy: headlines, body text, CTAs, forms, metadata, and documentation.

## Voice Rules (Conversion Copy)

**Style:**
- Direct, category-defining, operator language
- No poetry or puffery
- No buzzwords: "revolutionary", "cutting-edge", "ecosystem"
- Use short sentences
- Use contrast and economics

**Economic language (prioritize):**
- Revenue per fan
- Continuity
- Post-purchase engagement  
- Lifetime value
- Channel (not "platform" or "solution")

**Tech positioning:**
- Mention NFC only as a mechanism if needed
- Don't lead with tech
- Lead with outcomes, not features

## Objection Handling

### "This sounds complicated"
**Response:** No app required. No personal data collected. Fans just tap and go.

### "What if fans don't have NFC phones?"
**Response:** 95%+ of phones sold since 2018 have NFC. We don't use QR fallbacks because they compromise security and experience.

### "How much does this cost?"
**Response:** (Lead to conversation) Every team is different. Let's connect to explore if Vonga is right for you.

### "Will this work for our team?"
**Response:** We partner with teams of all sizes. White-glove service means we customize to your needs.

### "What about fan privacy?"
**Response:** No personal data collected. Fans control what they share. Privacy-first by design.
