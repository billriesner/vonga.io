# Hero Subhead: Post-Purchase Revenue Variations

**Core Concept:** Most merch stops earning at checkout. Vonga makes it keep working.  
**Why This Works:** Directly addresses the dormant asset problem with clear before/after

---

## Original Version (Baseline)

**Option 7 Original:**
> "Most merch stops earning at checkout. Yours keeps working. Every wearing = revenue. No app. No friction. Just results."

**Words:** 20  
**Strengths:** 
- Clear problem/solution
- Simple equation
- Removes friction
- Punchy ending

**Let's explore variations...**

---

## Variation Set 1: Punchy & Direct

### 7A - Ultra-Punchy
> "Merch stops earning at checkout. Make it keep working. Every time fans wear it, you earn."

**Words:** 16  
**Tone:** Bold, direct  
**Changes:**
- Removed "Most" (more assertive)
- "Make it keep working" (action-oriented)
- "Every time fans wear it, you earn" (clearer cause/effect)
- Removed friction removers (cleaner)

**Why Better:**
- More direct command (Make it)
- Clearer actor (fans)
- Simpler structure

---

### 7B - Stop/Start Format
> "Your merch stops earning at checkout. We make it start again. Every wearing = revenue. No app required."

**Words:** 17  
**Tone:** Problem/solution, clear  
**Changes:**
- "Your merch" (more personal)
- "Start again" (renewal metaphor)
- Kept equation
- Simple friction remover

**Why Better:**
- "Start again" = hopeful, actionable
- Personal (your merch)
- Clean structure

---

### 7C - Dead/Alive Format
> "Most merch dies at checkout. Yours keeps earning. Fans wear it 100+ times. You profit every time."

**Words:** 17  
**Tone:** Provocative, bold  
**Changes:**
- "Dies" (strong word)
- "Profit" instead of "revenue" (more direct)
- Quantified (100+ times)
