How It Works on Game Day

From production to ongoing engagement — a timeline.

Before Game Day

  1. Production: NFC chips embedded during manufacturing (adds ~$0.25/item)
  2. Configuration: Set up the tap experience in Vonga — exclusive content, sweepstakes entry, loyalty points
  3. Sponsor setup: Configure sponsor attribution if the item is branded

Game Day + Beyond

  1. Distribution: Hand out items at gates (same as today)
  2. First tap: In-seat or at home — fan unlocks content, you capture identity
  3. Ongoing engagement: Update content behind the tap throughout the season
  4. Data collection: Each tap adds to fan profile

Activation Options

Exclusive Content: Behind-the-scenes video, player messages, AR experiences

Loyalty Points: Award points for taps, redeemable for rewards

Sweepstakes Entry: Tap to enter for signed gear, tickets, experiences

Sponsor Offers: Branded offers from giveaway sponsors

The Data Trail

  • Re-engagement: Which fans tap weeks after getting the item?
  • Content consumption: What do they click after tapping?
  • Geographic spread: Where are your giveaway items traveling?
  • Repeat behavior: Who taps multiple times vs. once?

Fan Intelligence

Every tap enriches the fan profile. A fan who taps their rally towel 6 times over 3 weeks is more engaged than one who tapped once. Segment accordingly.
